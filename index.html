Paperbird - online auction platform

What is Paperbird?

Founded in 2010, Paperbird is an online auction platform where art collectors can interact - sell, buy, or exchange special and hard-to-find objects.

Why work with us?

Today our platform hosting auctions featuring 30,000 objects in more than 50 categories every month. Paperbird serves customers from all over the world and is available in 12 languages.

Our company

Headquartered in London, UK, Paperbird has 200 passionate employees - real art experts with decades of experience and knowledge - working across our European offices, serving customers around the world.
